Choosing your password

Your password safeguards access to your account. Select a phrase that is easy for you to remember while difficult for someone else to guess. A valid password must be between 8 and 128 characters long, and use at least one character from two of the three sets of allowed characters:

  • letters: a-z or A-Z
  • numbers: 0-9
  • special characters: !@#$%^&*().,

Passwords are case sensitive.
