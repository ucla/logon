Third Party Accounts

You are creating an account without a UCLA Identification Number (UID). Although usable with some UCLA applications, an account created without a UID is not generally eligible to receive campus services (email, network access).

If you are a student, alumnus, faculty, stafff, or contractor, please create a UCLA Logon with your UID to ensure access to univeristy resources.
