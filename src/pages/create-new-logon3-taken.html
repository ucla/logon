Choose your Logon ID

Your UCLA Logon ID must be between 3 and 15 characters in length and begin with a letter, following characters can be any combination of letters and numbers. A UCLA Logon ID is not case sensitive. Your UCLA Logon ID will be your main campus identifier and you'll use it to log in to most campus-wide applications. Choose carefully.

Choose your Password

Your password must be between 8 and 128 characters and contain at least two of the following three types of characters: letters, numbers, and the following special characters: !@#$%^&*().,. Passwords are case sensitive.
