Welcome to MyLogon

MyLogon lets you review your personal information, as well as manage security settings, privacy settings, and services associated with your UCLA Logon ID. In the future, if you have additional accounts associated with your UCLA Logon ID, you can also manage them here.

My Personal Information

Review your personal information and find links to resources that can help you update it.

Information to review includes:

Your Official Name, Preferred Name, Official Email, UID, etc.

My Accounts

Manage your account and security settings.

Account management options include:

Your UCLA Logon password, security settings such as Challenge Phrase and Multi-factor Authentication.

My Services

Review the services you have access to and manage service settings.

Service information includes:

Managing Bruin OnLine Mail Forwarding, Bruin OnLine Services Listing.
