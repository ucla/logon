Welcome to My Logon

My Logon lets you review your personal information, and allows you to manage security settings, privacy settings, and services associated with your UCLA Logon ID. If you have additional accounts associated with your UCLA Logon ID, you can manage them here as well.

My Personal Information

Review your personal information.

Information includes:

Your Official Name, Preferred Name, Official Email, UID, etc.

My Accounts

Manage your account settings and security.

Account settings include:

Your UCLA Logon password, security settings such as Challenge Phrases and Multi-factor Authentication.

My Services

Manage services you have access to.

Services include:

BruinOnline Mail Forwarding, BruinOnline Services Listing.
