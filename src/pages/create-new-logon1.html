Create a UCLA Logon ID: Confirm University Affiliation

Are you affiliated with UCLA?

You're affiliated with UCLA if you have a 9-digit UCLA University ID.

Users with a 9-digit UCLA University ID include:

  • New UCLA Students (including admitted applicants)
  • UCLA Applicants on the waitlist
  • Current and Former UCLA Students (including alumni)
  • UCLA Summer Session Only Students (new, current, or former)
  • UCLA Faculty or Staff Members
  • UCOP or UC Merced Staff Members
  • Contractors

I am affiliated with UCLA

You're not affiliated with UCLA if you do not have a 9-digit UCLA University ID.

Accounts created without a UCLA University ID number can be used with, but may not have full access to, many UCLA applications. Users with unaffiliated accounts generally aren't eligible to receive campus services.
