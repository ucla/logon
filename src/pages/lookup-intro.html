Are you affiliated with UCLA?

You're affiliated with UCLA if you have a 9-digit UCLA ID

Users with a 9-digit UCLA ID include:

  • New UCLA Students (including admitted applicants)
  • UCLA Applicants on the waitlist
  • Current and Former UCLA Students (including alumni)
  • UCLA Summer Session Only Students (new, current, or former)
  • UCLA Faculty or Staff Members
  • UCOP or UC Merced Staff Members
  • Contractors

You're not affiliated with UCLA if you do not have a 9-digit UCLA ID Number.
