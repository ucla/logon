Change Your Password

Your new password must be different from the old password, between 8 and 128 characters long, and use at least one character from two of the three sets of allowed characters:

  • letters: a-z or A-Z
  • numbers: 0-9
  • special characters: !@#$%^&*().,

Passwords are case sensitive.
