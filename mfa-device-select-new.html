Multi-Factor Authentication Enrollment

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) adds extra protection for your UCLA Logon account. With MFA enabled, you'll be asked to take an extra step to confirm your identity when signing in. This extra step prevents a hacker from accessing your account using a stolen password. Learn More about MFA.

Linked Devices:

None: MFA Disabled

You need to choose a device below to link to your UCLA Logon ID and enable Multi-Factor Authentication. We recommend using a Smartphone with a second device for backup in case the unbearable happens and life separates you from your constant companion. :emoji_sadface:

Link a Device:

Smartphone

We recommend using a smartphone. By installing the Duo Mobile App, you can quickly verify your identity by approving push notifications or use one-time pass-codes. Additionally, you can choose to get a SMS text message or phone call.

Tablet

This is the second preferred method. By installing the Duo Mobile App, you can quickly verify your identity by approving push notifications or use one-time pass-codes.

Cellular Phone

You can receive a SMS text message or phone call.

Landline

You can receive a phone call.
